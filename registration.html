Registration

A Special Note on Author Registration Policy

Each accepted paper/poster must be presented by one of its author(s) at the MobiHoc main conference or workshop.

To present a paper/poster at the conference or any one of the workshops, at least one author should register at the full (non-student) rates.

  1. Every paper must have a separate regular (i.e., non-student) registration. One registration is valid for ONE paper.
  2. Authors must register prior to the submission of camera-ready manuscript.
  3. The combined conference+workshop rate will cover ONE conference paper and ONE workshop paper by the same author.
  4. Authors must register prior to the submission of camera-ready manuscript.
  5. Poster/demo session authors may register at one-day registration rate if they are not attending the rest of the conference.
  6. Conference registration includes lunches, coffee break, reception on Tuesday Oct. 24 and banquet on Wednesday Oct. 25.
  7. Extra banquet tickets for guests are available for a fee of $150.
